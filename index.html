Hi! My name is Andrew Adams. I'm a teacher on the path to becoming a developer.

I graduated from the University of Kansas in 2012 with a degree in Music Education, and taught elementary and high school band in Kansas City for two years. I began learning code in my free time, and at some point, realized that I was more engrossed in what I was learning than what I was teaching. So I decided to make a change! I'm headed to Dev Bootcamp in June to start pursuing web development full time. I can't wait to begin a new journey with a host of bright classmates!

This is the third or fourth blog I have set up in the past month, but I plan on using this template for the remainder of my technical and cultural blog posts for DBC.
